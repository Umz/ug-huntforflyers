Play Report:

Farborne R-

The Farborne are a people from a remote desert land; a place too far for most to visit.

The Professor swears to improve their harsh life through any means, no cost too great, and no moral compass would restrict him.

So he began his experiments to mutate organisms and 'freeze' life forms; but his experiments require alot of 'material'. So he made many enemies, when he made his enemies into his 'material'.

How To Play-

Use the directional keys and select button or use the numbers on your remote control.

LEFT [4]Move Left
RIGHT [6]Move Right
UP [2]Interact (Read/Speak)
DOWN [8]Change Weapon
SELECT [5]Shoot (Hold)
